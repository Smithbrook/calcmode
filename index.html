About CalcMode

CalcMode was created to give traders and investors practical, trustworthy tools for the markets. Our calculators help you make sense of numbers, manage risk, and plan your next move—whether you’re just starting out or have years of experience.

We believe that financial tools should be accessible, accurate, and private. That’s why every calculation happens instantly in your browser, and your data never leaves your device. No sign-ups, no tracking, no hassle—just answers you can use right away.

Our suite includes calculators for percentage change, compound interest, position size, dividend yield, risk/reward, and options P/L. Each tool is designed to be clear, fast, and focused on real trading decisions.

CalcMode is a Smithbrook project, built by people who care about financial education and transparency. We want to empower you with knowledge and help you trade smarter, not harder.
