🍪 Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

CalcMode uses cookies sparingly and only for essential functionality:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off.
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by providing information about areas visited, time spent, and any issues encountered.
  • Preference Cookies: These remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We may use the following types of cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by CalcMode directly
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by third-party services we use (if any)

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or accept cookies through their settings.
  • Delete Cookies: You can delete cookies that have already been set.
  • Third-Party Tools: Various online tools can help you manage cookies across different websites.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of CalcMode. Some features may not work properly or may be unavailable if cookies are disabled.
